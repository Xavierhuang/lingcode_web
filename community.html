— For educators

Free for universities & schools.

If you teach mobile development, entrepreneurship, computer science, or design — LingCode is free for your classroom. No catch, no per-seat fee, no “academic edition” with features removed.

  • 01 Classroom license, free. Every student gets a Studio account for the duration of the course. Renewable each semester.
  • 02 Curriculum included. A 14-week iOS-development syllabus built around LingCode — slides, exercises, rubrics. Use it as-is or adapt.
  • 03 Guest instructors. Our team will guest-lecture in your class — virtually or in person — once per semester.
  • 04 Student showcase. Top student apps get featured on our homepage and Discord. A real shipping credential.
